About us


About Us coffewala, a part of Coffee Global Limited, is India’s favourite hangout for coffee and
conversations. Popularly known as coffewala, we strive to provide the best experience to our guests. Our
coffees are sourced from thousands of small coffee planters, who made us who we are today and
we're glad to be a part of their lives. We opened our first cafe in 2008 at Sader Road in Nagpur
– the youth and the young at heart immediately took to the cafe, and it continues to be one of the
most happening places in the city. coffewala to the youth is a “hangout” spot where they meet people,
make conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups of great coffee.It's been an
exciting journey since then to becoming the largest organised retail cafe chain in the country. We’re
also present in Austria, Czech Republic and Malaysia, so if your travel takes you there, do stop by our
outlets to get a taste from back home!
